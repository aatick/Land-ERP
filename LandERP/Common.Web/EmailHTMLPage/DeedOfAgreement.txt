<p align='center' style='text-align:center'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'><strong>WHEREAS, </strong>the <strong>COMPANY </strong>exhibited the proposed layout plan of <strong>&ldquo;PURBACHOL&rdquo; </strong>and thereafter on having proper investigation/verification of the documents/papers and after ascertaining the chain of title along with verifying the ownership of the concerned plot (mentioned hereinafter as Schedule&ndash; B), the <strong>ALLOTTEE </strong>is satisfied regarding the title of the land.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'><strong>AND WHEREAS </strong>the<strong> COMPANY</strong> allotted Schedule-B plot to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> at the request of the <strong>ALLOTTEE </strong>whereby the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> agreed to pay full consideration of the Schedule-B plot to the <strong>COMPANY </strong>along with development cost of the said plot.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'><strong>NOW, BOTH THE PARTIES ARE TO HAVE RECIPROCALLY BIND THEMSELVES BY THIS DEED OF AGREEMENT WHICH IS BEING SIGNED AND EXECUTED BY AND BETWEEN THE PARTIES ENUMERATING THE &ldquo;<u> TERMS AND CONDITIONS&rdquo; </u>OF THE TRANSACTION AS FOLLOWS:&nbsp;</strong></p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>1. That the price of the Schedule-B Plot measuring <strong>3.00</strong> (Three) katha is fixed at Tk. <strong>1350000.00</strong> (Taka Thirteen Lac Fifty Thousand Only) at the rate of Tk. 450000.00. (Taka Four Lac Fifty Thousand Only) per katha excluding the land development cost, utility connection cost and infrastructure development on the plot of the <strong>ALLOTTEE. </strong>The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall pay stamp duty; registration fee, Local Govt. Tax, VAT, At Sources Tax, Gain Tax and all other incidental cost and expenses to the <strong>COMPANY</strong> on demand and any violation of such will be deemed to be a violation of this Agreement.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>2. That the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> has paid TK. 30000.00/= (Taka Thirty Thousand Only) as Booking Money vide Cheque No. 4175838, dated - 23/12/2018 of TRUST BANK LTD. and the <strong>COMPAMY </strong>has issued Money Receipt being No. 14437, dated 02/02/2019.Thereafter the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> has paid TK. 5000.00/= (Taka Five Thousand Only) vide Cheque No. 3, dated - 02/02/2019 of AB BANK LTD. and the <strong>COMPAMY </strong>has issued Money Receipt being No. 0010000, dated 02/02/2019. The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> should pay registration fee, stamp, gain Tax, VAT, AIT, At sources tax and all other costs &amp; expenses.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>3. That all kinds of transaction/payment i.e. Booking Money, Down Payment, Installments, Closing Payment etc. shall be paid through Account Payee Cheque / Demand Draft/TT/Pay&nbsp; Order in favor of the <strong>COMPANY</strong> . In case of an emergency situation/urgent demand, the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall pay the equivalent cash amount to the authorized person of the <strong>COMPANY</strong>. The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall pay the same at the Marketing &amp; Sales Office of the <strong>COMPANY </strong>whereby the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> will be provided Money Receipt against the said deposited amount.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>4. That the time so fixed for payment for the land, its development cost, utility &amp; infrastructure cost shall be the essence of the contract.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>5.In case of a delay in payment, i.e. if delayed for more than 30 days from the due date as per Payment Schedule, a monthly interest at the rate of 2% shall be charged along with the payable amount. In that case <strong>COMPANY</strong> may cancel the allotment of the Plot for any such undue delay in payment of the installment for more than 90 days by serving 3 (Three) consecutive written notices within 60 days. After such cancellation of the allotment of the Plot, deposited amount by the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> will be refunded within 180 (one hundred and eighty) working days through installments after deducting 15% (fifteen Percent) service charge on the entire deposited amount.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>6. That the delay caused by irregular payment of installments by the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> may also delay the handover of the plot to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>. In such case <strong>COMPANY</strong> will not be responsible for the late hand over of plot.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>7. That the onus is upon the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> to ensure availability of sufficient fund in the Bank Account of his/her/them to honor the deposited Cheque and in case of any dishonored cheque (on each and every occasion) the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> will be liable to pay a service charge at the rate of 2% on the amount of the dishonored cheque as penalty. Three consecutive dishonor of Cheque in the entire duration of Payment Schedule, the <strong>COMPANY</strong> may cancel the Plot without giving any prior notice to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>8. That if the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> surrenders the allotment and/or desire to cancel the allotment, refund of the amount if any, to be made by the <strong>COMPANY</strong> to the <strong>ALLOTTEE </strong>shall be made after deduction of a service charge at the rate of 15% (Fifteen Percent) on the entire deposited amount within 180 (One Hundred Eighty) working days and by installments from the date of acceptance of the said surrender of allotment by the authority of the <strong>COMPANY</strong>.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>9.That the area of the plot(s) may vary on final measurement at the time of handing over physical possession to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>. In case of any such variation in measurement of the plot, the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall make further payment to the <strong>COMPANY</strong> for additional land, if any, and value of the said additional land will be determined by mutual understanding whereby the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> should pay the same. In case the area of the plot decreases, refund will be made by mutual understanding to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>10.That the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall bear all sorts of expenses in connection with the execution of the Deed of Conveyance for transfer of the Plot in his/her favor i.e. registration expenses (Non Judicial Stamp Duties, Gain Tax, Registration Fees, Local Govt. Tax, VAT, AIT, At sources Tax etc.) or any other charges, fees, duties that may be incurred in connection therewith and all incidental cost thereto and consequential thereupon.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>11.That the <strong>COMPANY</strong> reserves the right to change/modify/alter the layout plan for the greater interest of the project or for any notification/order by the Government or any other authority. In such case/situation, if the plot of the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> is dislocated, the <strong>COMPANY</strong> will try to reallocate a plot of same standard either within the same project.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>12.That the <strong>COMPANY</strong> reserves the right to change/modify/alter the layout plan for the greater interest of the project and for compliance of the Real Estate Development and Management Act, 2010 and Rules, 2011, The Non-Government Residential Land Project Development Rules, 2004, The Detailed Area Plan (DAP-2010), The Protection of Natural Water Reservoir Act, 2000 and Bangladesh Environment Protection Act, 1995 and the <strong>ALLOTTEE </strong>must abide by the said Acts, Rules and Laws as well.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>13. That in case, the project or any part of the project is abandoned due to any unpredictable or unavoidable circumstances or by an act of God, natural calamity, war, bar of the Government, and the <strong>COMPANY</strong> fails to deliver the allotted plot to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>, an alternative plot in any other place with another set of terms and conditions, as decided by the <strong>COMPANY</strong> will be offered to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>. If the newly proposed/offered plot is not acceptable by the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>, the <strong>COMPANY</strong> will refund the entire amount paid by the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> within 180 days through installments from the date of the declaration of such abandonment.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>14.That before registration of the sale deed, in case of changing of ownership of the plot, location of the plot etc., the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall have to pay TK. 10,000/= (Taka Ten Thousand only) per Plot to the <strong>COMPANY</strong>. But only in the case of changing of ownership of plot only to spouse, parents or children, the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> will not be charged. Only in case of death/demise of the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>, the nominee(s) will automatically get the allotted plot in his/her/their name or according to the transfer policy of the <strong>COMPANY</strong> and the nominee(s) will bear all the relevant cost related thereto.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>15.That the allotted plot will solely be used for the purpose of construction of residential building. The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> is forbidden to utilize his/her plot with a view to construct any commercial building or manufacturing unit.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>16.That the site-in-charge/Marketing &amp; Sales Office of the <strong>COMPANY</strong> should be informed in advance about the intention of the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> regarding any kinds of construction, its nature and possible date of commencement thereof. The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> is bound to show his/her allotment letter, all kinds of payment receipts, possession certificate, registered deed, approved plan etc. to the site-in-charge/Marketing &amp; Sales Office on demand.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>17.That the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall abide by the rules, regulation and practices of concerned Authorities as well as the <strong>COMPANY</strong> in relation to the construction of his/her house. He/she will be held responsible for any consequence arising out of his/her failure to follow construction Rules &amp; regulation of the authority concerned or in any manner of violation of rules of the concerned Authorities as well as the <strong>COMPANY</strong>. The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall not disturb or create any obstruction to the network of Utility Service of the project by his or her act of any unlawful/irresponsible behavior or by unauthorized construction whatsoever. However, if it is deemed fit or expedient, the <strong>COMPANY</strong> may, for use and purpose, permit a temporary work shed simply during the time of construction of planned building, which may likely to be sub-standard construction, in the project. The bending, hammering or cutting of M.S Rods etc. shall not, under any circumstances, be carried out on the roads, nor should it be used for mixing of concrete or any other construction activities. Storing of construction materials shall not be allowed on the road. No road or earth cutting is allowable outside the boundary of <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>. If the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> needs to use the nearby vacant plot temporarily for storing purposes during the time of construction of his/her house, he/she can only do it with the prior written permission of the owner of the neighboring plot. The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall not in any manner use or carry out any job in any part of land of the project which does not belong to his/her without prior permission of the <strong>COMPANY</strong>.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>18.That the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> will erect the boundary walls demarcating his/her/their plot within 3 (three) months after receiving the possession certificate with final measurement, any dispute arising out of failure to put boundary, may not be sustained.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>19.That the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> will maintain cleanliness in and around his/her house. Garbage should be disposed of in designated place and within the specified time to prevent conditions which may cause health hazard in the neighborhood.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>20.That the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall not keep domestic animals such as dogs, cows, goats or domestic birds like hens, ducks etc. in the plot after taking over possession of the plot.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>21.That the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall not do or organize or promote any such act, which may be termed as &lsquo;Anti-Social&rsquo;, or public nuisance and will lower and disgrace the sanctity of the community living in and around the project.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>22.That the network for supply of water, electricity and gas line will be installed after total development of the particular block of the project and the said utility services will be available to the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> after completion of the network of the block subject to the payment of all costs and incidental charges for utility connection from adjacent road to the plot by the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>23.That for the purpose of effective management, security &amp; maintenance of the project and the maintenance of common facilities &amp; privileges provided therein, the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>&amp; the <strong>COMPANY</strong> shall jointly form and constitute a beneficial co-operative Society and all the owners of plot within the project area shall compulsorily become members of that society. The Co-operative Society so formed and constituted shall be entitled with the management, security and maintenance of the project along with the maintenance of common facilities and privileges. The <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> should pay Tk.10,000/= (Taka Ten thousand) only per plot to the welfare fund of the project at the time of taking over possession of plot. The fund will finally be transferred to the Co-operating Society by the <strong>COMPANY</strong> as per the Co-operative Societies Act.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>24.That on receipt of entire payment the <strong>COMPANY</strong> shall handover physical possession of registered scheduled plot by 2023 A. D. in the name of the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong>. In case the allotted plot does not match the particulars as mentioned therein, the <strong>COMPANY</strong> will arrange a transfer deed specifying the actual final block, road, similar size of plot and location which shall also include cancellation of the earlier allotment and the cost of a such new allotment shall be borne by the <strong>COMPANY</strong> and the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> shall accept accordingly without raising any question whatsoever.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>25.That all disputes and differences concerning the validity, scope, meaning, construction of effect of this Agreement or any dispute or disagreement between the <strong>ALLOTTEE</strong> and the <strong>COMPANY</strong> as to any matter relating to this Agreement or the meaning of any stipulation therein or any other matter which cannot be settled by mutual discussion between the parties hereto shall be resolved through arbitration as per provision of the Arbitration Act, 2001.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>26.That this Deed of Agreement is made, prepared, typed and executed in exercise of the free and deliberate consent of both the parties here to and without any influence, coercion, threat and intimidation whatsoever from any side or quarter.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>27.That the Agreement has been made and signed in two exact original copies, one for each of the parties.&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p align='justify' style='text-align:justify'>28. That this agreement shall be governed by the existing Laws of the Land.&nbsp;</p>
